## Rules
- First 2 lines must hook — they show above "Show more"
- Timestamps: start at 0:00, at least 3 chapters, 10s+ apart
- Chapter labels: use searchable keywords, not "Part 1"
- Hashtags: 3-5 at the end, never in first lines
- Total: aim for 300-350 words
- Language: match the video's spoken language
